MY PERFORMANCES

LET'S COMMUNICATE!

Currently I am one of the main organizers for Thought Lounge, a college club soon to become an NGO.

A thought lounge is a formatted dialogue in which 4-6 participants each bring 1 topic, which is of utmost interest to him/her and which s/he thinks needs to be talked about, to a designated place. Each participant is given 12 minutes to facilitate a dialogue on the topic (that means you have the podium for 12 minutes. You can make it a full out presentation (powerpoint and all), facilitate an activity, or just write something up on the board and let the dialogue run. As long as at least part of it has an invitation to inquiry for group dialogue, you're good). After each topic is given its time, all topics are written up on the blackboard and 12 minutes are given to connect them all.

The purpose of it is to encourage people to communicate and talk about things that are important to them instead of having to make small talk with strangers. Imagine if you could just walk up to anybody and ask them what their opinions on feminism are? (You should probably know that I am one.) Or discuss modern day mobile communication and the way it impacts society. Our goal is to reach out to as many communities as many colleges as possible and introduce this new way of communication to build stronger bonds between people. So please, feel free to email or message me about ANYTHING you want to discuss and next time you see someone interesting on the street, just say hi to them :).
